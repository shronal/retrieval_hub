About Our Website

Our Lost and Found platform helps you list your lost items and find what others may have found. It's easy, quick, and designed to help the community connect over lost belongings.

My Project Inspiration

Colleges are the place where we often lose our belongings, from earphones to important documents. This platform was inspired by the need to help students recover their lost items more easily and efficiently.
